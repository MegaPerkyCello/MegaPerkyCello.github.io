### **What?**
- Plasma generators produce a tiny volume of plasma and have a geometry that hinders treatment of foods
- Developed plasma sterilizer that can be molded into any shape and produce a large volume of plasma to enable sterilization of foods


### **How?**
- Used **Fusion 360** to model a gas distributor customizable in shape and **3D-printed** it
- Designed a RF pulse-oscillator circuit using **Altium** to drive a flyback transformer and power the device
